About the Game

Dungeons and Dragons (D&D) is a tabletop role-playing game where players create their own characters and embark on adventures guided by a "Dungeon Master" (DM). The game is typically played with dice and a rulebook, and revolves around storytelling and imagination. Players use their characters' skills and abilities to navigate challenges, battle monsters, and solve puzzles, while the DM sets the scene and acts as the referee. D&D is a collaborative game that encourages creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving.

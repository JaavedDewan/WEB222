About the Site

This website is dedicated to my favorite non-electronic game, which is Dungeons and Dragons. I created this site as a showcase of my skills as a developer, and also to share my love for this game with others. I hope you enjoy exploring the site and learning more about the game.

About the Creator

My name is Jaaved Dewan, and I'm a student at Seneca College taking Computer Programming Analysis. I've been playing Dungeons and Dragons during the pandemic, and it has been a favorite pastime of mine ever since. I find this game to be really fun, unpredicatable and a great way to relax with your friends.
